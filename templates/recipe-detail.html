Chocolate Cake

Dessert By John Doe December 10, 2025

Ingredients

• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 2 cups sugar
• 3/4 cup cocoa powder
• 2 teaspoons baking soda
• 1 teaspoon baking powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 2 eggs
• 1 cup buttermilk
• 1 cup strong black coffee
• 1/2 cup vegetable oil
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans.

2. In a large bowl, stir together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.

3. Add eggs, buttermilk, coffee, oil, and vanilla. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.

4. Pour batter into prepared pans.

5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

6. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then remove to wire racks to cool completely.

7. Frost with your favorite chocolate frosting.
